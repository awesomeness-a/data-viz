Vizualizing World Cup data with Seaborn¶

For this project, we're gonna be exploring data from the Fifa World Cup from 1930-2014. This data is provided by Kaggle.

Using Seaborn, we'll create a series of plots that explore aggregates and distribution across the goals scored in World Cup games.

# create a bar chart visualizing how many goals were scored each year the World Cup was held between 1930-2014
sns.set_style('whitegrid')
sns.set_context('poster', font_scale=0.6)
f, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(12, 7))
ax = sns.barplot(
    data=df,
    x='Year',
    y='Total Goals')
ax.set_title('Total number of goals in FIFA cup')
plt.show()
